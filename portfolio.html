Drone Connections

In the Summer of 2023, I had the privilege of interning for Eve Vehicles, a drone startup affiliated with the University of Texas Center for Electromechanics (CEM). Working in a professional development environment was an entirely new experience for me. It wasn't a typical internship, I was given lots of freedom to solve the problems the team was facing, which was super exciting! This was difficult in the beginning because I didn't have much experience with the technologies they were using. I wanted to avoid weighing down a team that had much more work than they had employees (the engineering team was 3 people! <insert joke about startup life>). I am proud that I was able to become self-sufficient in the technologies being used, and made concrete contributions to the future of the project. I constructed the whole pipeline of flight log retrieval, which spanned from automating the log retrieval from the flight controller's companion computer to adding features to the UI that allowed for easy and efficient access to the logs.
